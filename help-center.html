Help Center

How do I get started with OmniMind24?

Simply register for an account, choose your plan, and start creating campaigns using our intuitive dashboard.

What AI services are included?

We integrate with 25+ AI services including OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, ElevenLabs, and many more.

How does the credit system work?

Credits are used for AI content generation. Each plan includes a monthly credit allocation. Additional credits can be purchased as needed.

What is the COSTAR framework?

COSTAR is our strategic campaign planning framework: Context, Objective, Strategy, Tone, Audience, and Results tracking.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. No long-term commitments required.

How secure is my data?

We use enterprise-grade encryption and AWS S3 for secure storage. Your data is protected with bank-level security.
