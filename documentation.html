Getting Started

Welcome to OmniMind24! This guide will help you create your first AI-powered marketing campaign in minutes.

1. Create Your Account

Sign up at omnimind24.com/register with your business email.

2. Set Up Your First Campaign

Navigate to the Campaign Creator and follow these steps:

  • Choose your campaign type (Email, Social Media, Content Marketing)
  • Define your target audience using COSTAR Framework
  • Select AI services (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, etc.)
  • Generate and review content
  • Deploy your campaign

Campaign Creation Guide

COSTAR Framework

OmniMind24 uses the COSTAR framework to ensure maximum campaign effectiveness:

  • Context: Define your business situation
  • Objective: Set clear campaign goals
  • Style: Choose brand voice and tone
  • Tone: Emotional direction (professional, friendly, urgent)
  • Audience: Target demographics and psychographics
  • Response: Desired customer action

Content Generation

Select from 25+ AI services to generate:

  • Email campaigns with subject lines and CTAs
  • Social media posts (LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram)
  • Blog articles and SEO content
  • Ad copy and landing pages
  • Video scripts and audio content
