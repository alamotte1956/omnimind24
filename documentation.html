Getting Started

Welcome to OmniMind24! This guide will help you get started with creating and managing AI-powered marketing campaigns.

Creating Your First Campaign

Follow these steps to create your first campaign:

  • Log in to your dashboard
  • Click "Create Campaign"
  • Fill in the COSTAR framework details
  • Select your AI services
  • Review and launch

Understanding COSTAR

COSTAR is our strategic framework for campaign planning:

  • Context: Define your campaign background
  • Objective: Set clear goals
  • Strategy: Plan your approach
  • Tone: Choose your brand voice
  • Audience: Define your target market
  • Results: Track performance metrics

AI Services
